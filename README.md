Assists in coordinating the duration of individual tasks, managing focus levels, and providing AI-driven decision support.
To use it, simply edit a task and assign scores for both urgency and importance; based on the designated weighting, the AI ​​will assist in prioritizing the tasks.
After selecting a task, click "Start Timer." If you need to step away temporarily, click "Pause Timer"; once the task is complete, click "End Timer."
If the underlying logic I designed is followed—specifically, if the level of focus exceeds 80%—fireworks will appear (rendered as GPU-based fireworks) once the task is completed.
Additionally, there are other AI-assisted data points to help you assess your level of focus!
